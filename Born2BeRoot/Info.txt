Apt v Aptitude:
	Aptitude is a higher-level package manager than apt
	Aptitude has vaster functionality and integrates functionalities of apt-get
	Aptitude offers visual interface, while apt is on the command-line
	Aptitude is more user-friendly than apt
	When facing a conflict, aptitude will offer solutions, while apt will not

Apparmor:
	security framework for Linux
	limits programs' capabilities
	such as access to files, network, raw sockets, etc.
	Apparmor is easier to maintain than SELinux
	SELinux is more secure than Apparmor
